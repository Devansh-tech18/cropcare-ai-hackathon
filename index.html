Leaf Blight Disease

94% Confident

Leaf blight is a fungal disease that causes brown spots and yellowing of leaves, commonly affecting crops during humid conditions.

Severity Level

Moderate

Recommended Treatment

Immediate Action

  • Remove affected leaves immediately
  • Improve air circulation around plants
  • Avoid overhead watering

Recommended Medicines

Copper Sulfate Fungicide

Apply 2-3 grams per liter of water

Every 7 days
Mancozeb 75% WP

Apply 2.5 grams per liter of water

Every 10 days

Prevention Tips

  • Maintain proper spacing between plants
  • Water at the base of plants, not on leaves
  • Apply organic compost to boost plant immunity
  • Monitor crops regularly for early detection
